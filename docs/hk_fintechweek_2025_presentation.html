Executive Summary

Key Takeaways

Hong Kong FinTech Week 2025 established AI integration and real-world asset tokenization as critical strategic directions for financial institutions. The event featured over 800 C-suite executives and unveiled major regulatory initiatives, including HKMA's "FinTech 2030" blueprint.

The convergence of artificial intelligence, regulated digital assets, and quantum-safe security creates both unprecedented opportunities and competitive threats for traditional banking models.
