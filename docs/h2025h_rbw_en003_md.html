HK Budget 2026 Key Points Relevant to Retail Banking and Wealth Management ### 3.1 Financial Services Development #### RMB Internationalization (Paragraphs 91-92) - **Doubling RMB Business Facility to RMB 200 billion** to support trade and cross-boundary business - More convenient foreign exchange quotations between RMB and regional currencies - Regular issuance of RMB bonds to enrich offshore RMB market - Exploring offshore RMB yield curve formation - Attracting high-quality issuers to increase RMB bond issuance **Implications for Hang Seng Bank:** - Expand RMB product offerings across deposits, loans, and investments - Develop RMB hedging and forex solutions for corporate and retail clients - Participate in RMB bond issuance and distribution #### Asset and Wealth Management Centre Development (Paragraph 98) - **Over 3,300 single-family offices** in Hong Kong - Tax regime enhancements including: - Expanding "fund" scope to cover specific funds-of-one - Classifying digital assets, precious metals, commodities as qualifying investments - Implementation from year of assessment 2025/26 **Implications for Hang Seng Bank:** - Develop dedicated family office service teams - Create digital asset investment solutions - Enhance precious metals and commodities trading capabilities #### REIT Market Development (Paragraph 99) - Seeking inclusion of REITs under mutual-market access - Amendment bill to enable REIT privatisation/restructuring - Stamp duty waiver for non-residential property transfers into REITs **Implications for Hang Seng Bank:** - Develop REIT-focused investment products - Provide REIT financing and advisory services - Create property-to-REIT structuring solutions ### 3.2 Fintech and Digital Assets (Paragraphs 104-110) #### Digital Asset Licensing Regime - Bill to establish licensing for digital asset dealing and custodian service providers - Fiat-referenced stablecoin licensing regime with first licenses to be issued - SFC to enhance digital asset market liquidity and facilitate more products for professional investors - SFC accelerator to expedite market innovation **Implications for Hang Seng Bank:** - Apply for digital asset dealing and custody licenses - Develop stablecoin payment and settlement solutions - Create digital asset investment products for qualified investors #### Project Ensemble (Paragraph 109) - HKMA launched pilot for tokenised deposits and digital assets - 24/7 settlement capability development - Local standards development for interoperability **Implications for Hang Seng Bank:** - Participate in Project Ensemble pilot programs - Develop tokenised deposit products - Explore digital asset collateralized lending ### 3.3 Support Measures for Citizens and Businesses (Paragraphs 279-280) #### Tax Relief Measures - Salaries tax reduction: 100% up to $3,000 ceiling (2025/26) - Profits tax reduction: 100% up to $3,000 ceiling - Basic allowance increase: $132,000 to $145,000 (from 2026/27) - Child allowance increase: $130,000 to $140,000 - Dependent parent allowance increase **Implications for Hang Seng Bank:** - Develop tax-season promotional campaigns - Create tax-optimized investment and savings products - Offer tax payment financing solutions ### 3.4 Green Finance (Paragraphs 202-204) - Continued sustainable bond issuance - Hong Kong Taxonomy for Sustainable Finance refinement - Green transition planning guidance for banks - Exploration of Green Technology Projects Accelerator - Sustainable finance and investment cumulative total: $495.6bn (up from $393.6bn in 2024) **Implications for Hang Seng Bank:** -
