Policy Correlation

HK Budget 2026 Strategic Alignment

Key initiatives from the Hong Kong Budget 2026 that create opportunities for Hang Seng Bank's retail banking and wealth management business.

¥

RMB Internationalization

Doubling of the RMB Business Facility to RMB 200 billion to support trade and cross-boundary business.

  • More convenient RMB forex quotations
  • Regular RMB bond issuance
  • Offshore RMB yield curve development
  • Cross-boundary transaction growth
🏢

Family Office Hub

Over 3,300 single-family offices in Hong Kong with enhanced tax regime for wealth management.

  • Expanded "fund" scope for tax concessions
  • Digital assets as qualifying investments
  • Precious metals & commodities inclusion
  • Effective from year of assessment 2025/26
🪙

Digital Assets

Comprehensive regulatory framework for digital asset innovation and licensing regime establishment.

  • Licensing for digital asset dealing & custody
  • Stablecoin licensing regime launch
  • SFC accelerator for market innovation
  • Project Ensemble for tokenized deposits
💵

Tax Relief Measures

100% salaries tax reduction up to $3,000 ceiling for year of assessment 2025/26.

  • Basic allowance increase to $145,000
  • Child allowance increase to $140,000
  • Dependent parent allowance enhancements
  • Profits tax reduction for businesses
🌱

Green Finance

Consolidating Hong Kong's status as an international green finance centre through sustainable initiatives.

  • Hong Kong Taxonomy for Sustainable Finance
  • Green transition planning guidance for banks
  • Green Technology Projects Accelerator
  • Sustainable bond issuance continuation
🏘️

REIT Market Development

Seeking REIT inclusion under mutual-market access with stamp duty waivers for property transfers.

  • REIT privatisation/restructuring enablement
  • Stamp duty waiver for non-residential transfers
  • Enhanced REIT market liquidity
  • Cross-border REIT investment facilitation
