üí° Final Recommendations

As Head of Hang Seng Group Retail Banking, I recommend:

  1. Face competition without panic: HSBC's strength is expected; Hang Seng has unique advantages (local expertise, agile decision-making, HSBC synergy)
  2. Focus on differentiation, avoid head-on competition: Don't compete with HSBC on scale, but build advantages in service depth, customer relationships, and cross-border convenience
  3. Accelerate transformation to capture the window: The historic opportunity for Hong Kong to become a global wealth hub is time-limited; accelerate wealth management transformation
  4. Proactively drive synergy: During privatization, actively seek strategic positioning favorable to Hang Seng and maximize synergy benefits
  5. Risk-first, prudent operations: While expanding, strictly control commercial real estate risks and maintain adequate capital buffers
