Section 05

HK Budget 2026 Implications

Key initiatives and strategic opportunities from the Hong Kong Budget 2026-27

¥

RMB Internationalization

RMB Business Facility doubled to RMB 200bn. Enhanced forex quotations for RMB/regional currencies. Regular RMB bond issuance to enrich offshore market.

🏢

Family Office Hub

3,300+ single-family offices in Hong Kong. Tax regime enhancements for funds-of-one. Digital assets, precious metals now qualifying investments.

🏠

REIT Market Development

Mutual market access inclusion for REITs. Amendment bill for REIT privatization/restructuring. Stamp duty waiver for non-residential transfers.

🔐

Digital Asset Licensing

Licensing regime for dealing and custodian services. Fiat-referenced stablecoin licenses to be issued. SFC accelerator for market innovation.

🪙

Project Ensemble

HKMA pilot for tokenized deposits and digital assets. 24/7 settlement capability development. Local standards for interoperability.

🌱

Green Finance

Sustainable finance cumulative: $495.6bn. Hong Kong Taxonomy refinement. Green transition planning guidance for banks. Green Technology Accelerator.

Tax Relief Measures

Salaries tax reduction: 100% up to $3,000 ceiling. Profits tax reduction: 100% up to $3,000 ceiling. Basic allowance increased from $132,000 to $145,000 from 2026/27. Child allowance increased to $140,000. Strategic opportunity for tax-season promotional campaigns and tax-optimized investment products.
